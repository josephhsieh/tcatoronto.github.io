Welcome to TCAT

多倫多台灣同鄉會

Taiwanese Canadian Association of Toronto (TCAT), the largest general-purpose all-ages Taiwanese organization in the Greater Toronto Area, was established back in 1963 and later registered as a non-profit organization in the Province of Ontario in 1975.

The mandates of TCAT are:
1)   to assist with Taiwanese immigrants, who reside in the GTA, in adapting to the environment of Canada
2)   to gather members to serve local communities within the GTA
3)   to provide methods of communications between members and all three levels of Canadian governments
4)   to maintain and promote Taiwanese culture to the Canadian society

TCAT, in addition to hosting cultural, social, and sport events throughout the year, also publishes three issues of TOGETHER magazines each year as well as broadcasts monthly community newsletters. Three divisions, namely the Formosan Cup Division (tFCD) and the Public Affair Division (tPAD), and the Talent (tTalent), were established in 1974, 2009, and 2014 respectively to focus on special events that further promote multiculturalism.

TCAT Cabinet

TCAT cabinet is the body of high-ranking TCAT officials, typically consisting of the top leaders of Taiwanese Canadian communities in Greater Toronto Area.
